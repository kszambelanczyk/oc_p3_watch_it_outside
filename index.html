Boston Outdoor Film Festival

‘Watch it outside Boston edition’

About the Festival

‘Watch it outside Boston edition’ is a free and open to everyone, one of the best outdoor film festivals. Started by Clara Dunn, the founder of Watch it outside events Co., whose mission is to promote good cinema for a wide audience of all ages. This time the the event will take place at the beautiful Boston Public Garden from August 5th through August 8th 2019. We have the best hits and classics for you, so check the program and quickly register, because there are only 1500 seats available.

Program

The Godfather

Pulp fiction

Two imprisoned men bond over a number of years, finding solace and eventual redemption through acts of common decency.

The Godfather: Part II

Forrest Gump

Starts at: 10pm
